Tween

Tweens can be applied to almost any object within the engine but should not be used for NPC or Bullet movement since these are controlled within the physics system.

Apply a tween

 createjs.Tween.get(target).to({x:100}, 500, createjs.Ease.linear);

Remove a tween

createjs.Tween.removeTweens(object);
