Factions

Factions are a mainstay in the game and all NPC's even the player characters belong to a faction.

All factions in the game

_BLUEPRINTS.FACTIONS
Note: Modifying the FACTIONS blueprints before the game is loaded is safe and would just change that faction in game. Once in game, modifying this data would have unwanted side effects.

Get faction
const sinFaction = game.session.factions.getFaction("sincorp");

Get player reputation

game.session.factions.getReputation('faction_sincorp');

Add player reputation

game.session.factions.removeReputation('faction_sincorp', 5)

Add reputation between factions

game.session.factions.addReputationFor('faction_sincorp', 'faction_yamakai', 50)

Check if two factions are hostile to eachother

game.session.factions.factionHostile('faction_sincorp', 'faction_nomad')
